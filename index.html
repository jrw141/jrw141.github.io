Bio

Welcome to my website!

My name is Josh White and I'm a junior at the University of Miami, majoring in broadcast journalism and sport administration.

I'm currently the Sports Director and lead student play-by-play voice of Hurricanes' football, baseball, volleyball and men's and women's basketball at WVUM 90.5. I also work as a freelance broadcaster, calling a variety of sports for Nova Southeastern University and Florida International University.

I spent the 2018 summer as the play-by-play voice of the Orleans Firebirds in the Cape Cod Baseball League.

Thank you very much for visiting my website and I hope you enjoy!
